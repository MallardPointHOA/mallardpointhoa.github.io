AT THE EDGE OF EFFICIENCY

Neighborhood Profile

Known for its extensive recreation, spectacular scenery and prime access to work hubs Seattle, Bellevue, Renton, and Redmond with its placement along the I-90 corridor, Issaquah is a true gem of the Pacific Northwest. The residents of Issaquah enjoy yearlong cultural activities ranging from the famous Salmon days to one of the regions best farmers markets to the always popular Issaquah Fish Hatchery and thriving art scene. Issaquah also boasts an award winning school district, extensive shopping, a classically preserved quaint downtown, and some of the best hiking and biking in the state while 20 minutes from Seattle or the mountains.
